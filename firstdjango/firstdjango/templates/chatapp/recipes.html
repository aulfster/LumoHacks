Recipes: Sorted by Nature of Illness

Everyone's gotta eat - that's the motto here at CANEATS. In health and sickness, we hope that food continues to be a positive experience for everyone. Nourish yourself and take the time to treat yourself.

Whether you're going through a strict diet before your treatment plan, are currently on your treatment plan, or you've beat the odds and are on the road to recovery, your friends here at CANEATS are happy to share the recipes that keep the fight in the fighter.

Nervous about the radioiodine ablation while having to worry about the low-iodine diet? Problems beating the crave? Have no fear! There are hundreds of thousands of people going through the same thing as you are. And here are their suggestions!

Soft Foods to Eat Post-Thyroidectomy

Neck pain making you down? Difficult swallowing shouldn’t prevent you from the road to recovery. Enter into a world of delicious smoothies to make you feel 100% better.

Most people complain about weight gain after thyroid cancer treatment. You don’t have to be one of them. Active lifestyle and delicious healthy food can help with your decreased metabolism. Perhaps you can share your secret tip with the world too while you are at it.
