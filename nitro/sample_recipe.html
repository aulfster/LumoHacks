Oatmeal With Blueberries, Sunflower Seeds, and Agave

Directions

  1. Boil water with oats.
  2. Cook for about 5 minutes over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Top with blueberries and sunflower seeds.
  4. Finish off with a drizzle of agave nectar.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup of blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon of sunflower seeds
  • 1 tablespoon of agave nectar
